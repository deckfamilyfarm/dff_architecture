# Site Planning Goals

## 1) Visualization goals
- View the site plan and visualize key elements (barns, building locations, roads, etc.).
- Use layers to guide siting decisions, especially for the house.

## 2) Decision layers & attributes to track
### Light
- Maximize afternoon light.

### Smell-shed (animal barns)
- Red zone: within 250 feet of barns.
- Orange zone: within 500 feet of barns.

### Fly-zone (animal barns)
- Red zone: within 500 feet of barns.
- Orange zone: within 1,000 feet of barns.
